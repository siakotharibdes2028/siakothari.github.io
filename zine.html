Zine

“Chalo Farva Jaiye!” is a visual zine that explores how Gujaratis experience travel abroad—not just as tourists, but as cultural carriers. The project captures the humor, habits, emotions, and identity shifts that come with global travel, while staying deeply rooted in Gujarati culture.

The zine is designed as a playful yet observational narrative. It combines illustration, typography, and satire to highlight relatable moments—such as food habits, shopping calculations, gift-giving pressure, and constant comparison with India. Each spread acts like a snapshot of the Gujarati travel mindset, blending wit with cultural insight.

Visually, the zine draws inspiration from travel artifacts like passports, stamps, maps, and souvenirs. Traditional Gujarati patterns are merged with global travel aesthetics to reflect the intersection of “home” and “abroad.” Bright colors, textured backgrounds, and bold typography make the zine engaging and expressive.
