Card Deck Cards

Font Hunt is an interactive card deck game designed to make learning typography engaging and enjoyable. The game challenges players to identify typefaces using clues based on font anatomy, such as stems, counters, terminals, and proportions, encouraging careful observation and deeper understanding of letterforms.

The concept is inspired by the typeface Futura, known for its clean, geometric structure. This influence is reflected in the overall design of the game, where layouts, shapes, and compositions are built using simple geometric forms. To contrast this structured approach, the visual theme is vibrant and colourful, adding a sense of playfulness and making the learning experience more accessible.

By combining elements of education and gameplay, Font Hunt transforms technical typographic knowledge into an interactive activity. It helps players develop visual sensitivity, improve recognition skills, and build a stronger understanding of type in a fun and memorable way.
