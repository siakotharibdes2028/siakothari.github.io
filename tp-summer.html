Typography Poster Summer

This typography poster explores the theme of summer through a vibrant and expressive visual language.The central concept is built around shaping the typography into the form of an ice cream, a strong and instantly recognizable symbol of summer. By arranging and manipulating letterforms to create this shape, the design blurs the line between text and image, allowing typography to communicate both visually and conceptually.

Bright and bold colours are used to enhance the feeling of warmth, joy, and refreshment associated with summer. The composition feels lively and dynamic, capturing the carefree and fun nature of the season. Through this approach, the poster highlights how typography can go beyond its functional role and become a creative tool for storytelling, resulting in a visually engaging and memorable representation of summer.
