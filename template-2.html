Wedding Invites Caricature

This wedding invitation uses caricatures to create a personalized and elegant representation of the couple. The design focuses on a stylized yet subtle depiction of their likeness, avoiding exaggeration and maintaining a sophisticated tone. Each function—Haldi, Mehendi, Sangeet, and the Wedding—is thoughtfully incorporated within a cohesive layout. The overall concept blends individuality with refinement, resulting in a visually distinctive and meaningful invitation.
